Sky Dash V2

This version turns the old dodge game into a full survivor run. You level up, draft talents, evolve them into stronger forms, fight a boss at the end, and earn Echoes for deep runs. Echoes buy permanent upgrades for future attempts.

The talent pool has 80 cards. Some synergize, some lock out others, and several evolve when you hit the right build path.

Survive until the final boss arrives at 2:00. Beat it to clear the run. You only gain Echoes from important milestones like major level marks and boss clears.
